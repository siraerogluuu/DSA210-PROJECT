# DSA210-PROJECT

For this project, I am interested in understanding why some of the TikTok videos get more views than other ones.Moreover, I want to focus especially on the timing of the post, for example whether posting in the morning, afternoon or evening makes any difference on the like or view amount. In my opinion most people look at likes or comments but timing might also have an effect.I will use a dataset from Kaggle containing data such as views, likes, comments, video length, and hashtags for TikTok videos. Furthermore, after selecting a suitable dataset, I will download it and then apply the necessary processing steps before starting my analysis. I'am planning to create more additional variables in order to improve the dataset. For instance, I could group the sharing time by part of the day and check whether the video was shared on a weekday or weekend. Finally, I will examine the data for any related patterns. In conclusion, I will try to understand whether the posting time is related to higher views or any engagement.
